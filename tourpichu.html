How To Reach

  • By air:
    The nearest airport to Machu Picchu is:

    1. Alejandro Velasco Astete International Airport (Cusco)


  • By Rail:
    Take a train to Aguas Calientes (the town near Machu Picchu) from Cusco. From Aguas Calientes, a bus or a hike takes you to Machu Picchu.

    1. From Cusco to Aguas Calientes by train


  • By Hiking:
    You can also reach Machu Picchu by hiking the famous Inca Trail, which offers a breathtaking journey through the Andes.
