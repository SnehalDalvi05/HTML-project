How To Reach

  • By air:
    The nearest airports to the Great Wall of China are:

    1. Beijing Capital International Airport
    2. Beijing Daxing International Airport


  • By Rail:
    High-speed trains connect Beijing to major cities. You can reach the Great Wall from Beijing by bus or taxi.

    1. From Beijing


  • By Road:
    The Great Wall is easily accessible by road. You can hire a taxi or take a bus from Beijing to reach different sections of the wall.

    1. Mutianyu section - 73 km from Beijing
    2. Badaling section - 80 km from Beijing
    3. Jinshanling section - 154 km from Beijing
