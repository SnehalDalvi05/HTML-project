How To Reach

  • By air:
    The nearest airport to Chichen Itza is:

    1. Cancun International Airport


  • By Road:
    Chichen Itza is well connected by road. You can take a bus or hire a taxi from nearby cities.

    1. From Cancun - approximately 200 km
    2. From Merida - approximately 120 km

  • By Air:
    Chichen Itza, a renowned archaeological site in Mexico, doesn't have direct rail connections. However, you can use a combination of rail and air travel to reach it.

    1. Fly to Cancun: The nearest major airport is Cancun International Airport (CUN). From most international locations, you can find direct flights to Cancun.
    2. From Cancun to Chichen Itza: Once in Cancun, you have a few options:
      • Domestic Flight: There are small airlines offering flights from Cancun to nearby cities like Merida, which is relatively close to Chichen Itza. From Merida, you can take a bus or hire a taxi to reach Chichen Itza.
      • Private Charter or Helicopter: Some companies offer charter services or helicopter tours from Cancun directly to Chichen Itza.

  • By Rail:
    Mexico doesn't have a comprehensive passenger rail network that connects major tourist sites. Therefore, there's no direct train service to Chichen Itza.

    1. From Merida (Closest Major City): Another common route is to travel to the city of Merida in Mexico's Yucatan state, which is relatively close to Chichen Itza. From Merida:
    2. Bus: There are frequent bus services from Merida to Chichen Itza. The journey takes around 2-3 hours, depending on the type of bus and stops
    3. Private Taxi or Car: Taxis or rental cars are also an option, providing flexibility in your travel schedule
