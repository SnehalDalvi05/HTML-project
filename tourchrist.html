How To Reach

  • By air:
    The fastest and most comfortable way to reach Christ the Redeemer is to travel by air via

    1. Rio de Janeiro International Airport
    2. Santos Dumont Airport


  • By Rail:
    For people who don’t have the budget to fly, traveling by train is a convenient option.

    1. From Rio de Janeiro
    2. From São Paulo


  • By Road:
    Rio de Janeiro is well connected to major cities in Brazil:

    1. São Paulo - 430 km
    2. Brasília - 1,150 km
    3. Belo Horizonte - 440 km
    4. Salvador - 1,680 km
    5. Recife - 2,300 km
