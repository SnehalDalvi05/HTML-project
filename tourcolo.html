How To Reach

  • By air:
    The fastest and most convenient way to reach the Colosseum is to travel by air via

    1. Rome Fiumicino Airport
    2. Rome Ciampino Airport


  • By Rail:
    If you prefer traveling by train, there are excellent rail connections to Rome from various European cities.

    1. From Florence
    2. From Milan


  • By Road:
    Rome has a well-connected road network, and you can reach the Colosseum by road from nearby cities:

    1. Florence - 275 km
    2. Naples - 230 km
    3. Milan - 575 km
    4. Venice - 530 km
    5. Amalfi Coast - 270 km
